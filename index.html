Management Succession Uncertainty

Worried who’ll run things post-sale? We map your leadership team, spot gaps, and build a transition plan that keeps your people—and your buyers—confident.

Deal Structure Misalignment

Clashing price expectations, transition terms or stakeholder goals stall negotiations. We align everyone around a clear valuation, structure and timeline for a win-win.
